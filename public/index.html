Projects
Neural Network for Time Series Financial Data — LSTM

Brief:

I gathered data, cleaned the data, and my neural network made sense of it.

Detailed:

I used Alpaca’s API to gather eight years of time series data, to which I applied technical and simple indicators. I then had to clean my data, as not every stock had been on the market for eight years. Which I achieved by prepending tensors of zeros to the rear of my rank-two tensors (the enclosing tensor was rank-three), which meant that any days during which it wasn’t publicly listed would be ignored. Which meant that my LSTM could take a time series of any length as input and produce a reliable prediction.
