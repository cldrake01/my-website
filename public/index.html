Neural Network for Time Series Financial Data — LSTM

Brief:
  • I collected and cleaned time series data, employing a neural network to extract meaningful insights and patterns.

Detailed:
  • I utilized Alpaca's API to collect eight years' worth of time series data. I then applied various technical and simple indicators to this dataset. To ensure data consistency, I performed data cleaning since not all stocks had an eight-year market history. For data cleaning, I adopted an approach where I prepended tensors of zeros to my rank-two tensors (the enclosing tensor was rank-three). This technique effectively filters out periods during which a stock wasn't publicly listed. The LSTM model I developed is capable of processing time series data of variable lengths and consistently generates reliable predictions.
